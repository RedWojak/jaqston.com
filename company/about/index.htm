About us

Jaqston Trading Limited is a large trading company specializing in supplying cocoa products, cocoa beans, confectionery fats, sesame seeds to Russia confectionary factories.

Jaqston company was founded in 2006.Our headquarter is located in Cyprus, Nicosia.

During years of work our company has managed to gain profound knowledge of our customers and their needs, of the markets of raw materials and their characteristics, established favorable and stable relationships with leading manufacturers of raw materials for confectionery.

Our company buys raw materials in Europe, Asia, Africa and Americas. Jaqston's main partners are such esteemed companies like Olam International, Theobroma, Euromar, Wuxi Huadong, Aarhus Karlshamn, Wilmar, Loders Croklaan, Fuji Oil Europe and others.

Our company's list of customers includes largest Russian confectionery factories such as the "Red October", "Rot Front", "Babaevsky Confectionery Concern", "Russian Chocolate", "Factory of Chocolate Masses" and others.

The company provides services on organizing logistics by all modes of transport. Our main logistical partners are TELS, DFDS Logistics, Bruhn Spedition, Contimer, Katoen Natien and other major logistics operators.
