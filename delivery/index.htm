During the years of work company Jaqston Trading Limited has gained large experience in coordinating deliveries of goods by means of different types of transport.


Through and thanks to wide network of international partners all over the world our company provides our customers with right-in-time forwarding services, including delivery by railway and sea transport and organizes good’s insurance during transportation and safekeeping storage in Tallinn’s logistic hub (Estonia)


At the moment the geography of shipments includes different countries and directions such as:


  • FROM Netherlands, Germany, UK, Belgium, Sweden, Denmark, Spain, Poland di-rectly to Russia by motor vehicles and container train;

  • FROM Cote dIvoire, Ghana, Nigeria, Malaysia, Indonesia, China, India to the port of Tallinn (Estonia) by marine vessels/ containers, and then to Russia by road /container train/ railway wagons.

Incoterms Rules

INCOTERMS 2010
Incoterms 2010 is an international law recognized by government agencies, law firms and traders worldwide as the interpretation of the applicable international trade terms.
Revision entered into force on 01 January 2011.
The scope of Incoterms 2010 (Incoterms 2010) covers the rights and obligations of the parties under the contract of sale in terms of supply of goods (terms of delivery of goods).

ICC Russia national committee
6 may 2000 the ICC world Council formally accepted Russia into the world Business Organization and unanimously approved the establishment of non-Profit partnership "Russian national Committee of International chamber of Commerce - world business organization" (ICC Russia). Thus, Russian companies received the opportunity to actively participate in shaping the rules and principles of international business based on the needs and problems of their country on equal terms with foreign partners. Currently under the auspices of the ICC Russia Arbitration Commission works, as well as the Commission on banking technique and practice. ICC Russia's mission is to strengthen the position of Russian business in the global business community and the support of the Russian companies in the context of new challenges and opportunities of globalization.
